Our functionality

All functions gathered in one service
to help businesses grow

01Monitoring

Monitoring and processing new comments, messages and mentions from social networks, analysis of sentiment and response speed of managers.

02Account statistics

We have collected statistics of all popular social networks for you in one place.

Hourly picking on your and competitors’ accounts for a detailed analysis of social networks and search for growth points with many derivatives. For example, rating of profiles depending on subscriber and likes growth or the effectiveness of content categories and more.

03Auto Posting

Monitoring and processing new comments, messages and Schedule posts to all your accounts one time a month in advance and we will publish the content, collect comments and statistics on it. With our built-in calendar, photo and text editor your work will become much easier.

04Reports

Export reports for your and competitor’s account in PDF, XLS and PPTX. Reports can be edited: you can hide unnecessary sheets, add comments and branding for the client.

05KPI

Set the most popular KPIs such as likes, ER, ERR and reach for each account and track their progress in real time.

06Blogger checking

Detecting dishonest bloggers so that you won’t let your budget go down the drain. We analyze subscribers’ activity, dynamics of growth of likes and subscribers. We can easily detect cheat.

07Direct

You can work with messages in Instagram Direct from your PC as there are many opportunities. You may reply to a user's comment in Direct, tag messages by topic and put notes for a team work, use templates for replies and appoint a responsible operator in Direct. This function mostly fits for commercial use.
